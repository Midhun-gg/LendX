About LendX

Our Story

LendX is a revolutionary peer-to-peer lending platform designed specifically for students and faculty on campus. Our mission is to create a trusted community where members can share resources, build connections, and support each other's needs.

Founded by a team of passionate individuals who understand the challenges of campus life, LendX provides a secure and convenient way to borrow and lend items within your academic community.

Our Team

LendX was developed by a dedicated team of innovators:

  • Midhun - Co-founder and Technical Lead
  • Diya - Co-founder and UX Designer

Together, we're committed to making campus life more sustainable and connected through shared resources.

Our Mission

We believe in creating a sustainable future through sharing. By connecting people who have items with those who need them, we're reducing waste, building community, and making campus life more affordable and accessible for everyone.
